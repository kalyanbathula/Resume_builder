SOFTWARE DEVELOPER

A Software Developer is a professional who designs, develops, and maintains software applications. They work on a wide range of software projects, including desktop applications, mobile apps, web applications, and more. They may also be involved in the entire software development lifecycle, from conceptualization to deployment.

The growth of software developers has been significant in recent years and is expected to continue in the future. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the employment of software developers is projected to grow 21% from 2019 to 2029, much faster than the average for all occupations.
