AI ENGINEER

An AI engineer is a professional who is responsible for designing, developing, and deploying artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) systems. This includes tasks such as creating and implementing algorithms, building and training models, and integrating AI and ML systems with other software and hardware.
